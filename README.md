# 🎓 DIU CoverGen v2.0

A high-performance, mobile-responsive **Document Scanner & Cover Page Generator** specifically designed for **Daffodil International University (DIU)** students.

## 🚀 Live Demo
**https://diu-covergen.vercel.app/**

## ✨ Key Features
* **AI Magic Color Scanner:** Turn photos of handwritten pages into professional digital documents with enhanced contrast and vivid colors.
* **Hybrid Assignment Merge:** * **PDF Mode:** Upload and merge an existing assignment PDF with zero quality loss.
    * **Photos Mode:** Upload multiple JPG/PNG images and convert them into a professional A4 PDF instantly.
* **Smart Drag-and-Drop Reordering:** Easily fix the sequence of your uploaded pages without restarting—just grab and move.
* **Smart Forms:** Pre-configured templates for Theory Assignments, Lab Reports, Lab Finals, and more.
* **Auto-Save (LocalStorage):** Remembers your Identity (Name, ID, Batch, Section) so you only type them once.
* **Dynamic Font Scaling:** Intelligently adjusts text size for long names or titles to ensure a perfect single-page A4 layout.
* **Mobile Optimized:** Engineered to prevent blank second pages and ensure smooth performance on all mobile browsers.
* **High-Resolution Export:** Uses 4x canvas scaling for crisp, professional-grade printing.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
* **React + Vite**
* **Tailwind CSS**
* **Lucide React** (Icons)
* **html2pdf.js** (PDF Generation)
* **pdf-lib** (High-fidelity Merging & Image-to-PDF)
* **HTML5 Canvas API** (Image Processing & Filters)
